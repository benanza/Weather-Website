Latitude vs. Humidity

This plot demonstrates that there is a noticeable correlation between humidity and latitude, but rather that being linear or logarithmic, it is clustered within certain ranges of latitude. Generally speaking, between latitudes of -40 and 0 degrees, as well as between 40 and 70 degrees, the humidity sits between 50% and 100%. Between latitudes of 0 and 40 degrees, the humidity ranges from 5% to 85%.
